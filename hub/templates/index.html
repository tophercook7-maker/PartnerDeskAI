Video Partner 🎬 Local-business video creation + marketing campaigns —

Start Here

  1. Fill out your Business Profile first.
  2. Choose what kind of video help you need.
  3. Generate a draft package.
  4. Review and edit the package.
  5. Approve it only when it looks ready.
  6. Mark it Used after you actually post or use it somewhere.
Important: Video Partner does not publish anything for you. It only prepares content for review.

Not sure where to start?

  • Full Video Campaign — if you want the complete package.
  • 30-Day Video Calendar — if you need ideas.
  • Short Script — if you already know the topic.
  • Ad Script — if you are promoting an offer.
  • Shot List — if you are ready to film.
  • Caption Pack — after you already have a video idea.
  • Titles / Description / Hashtags — when preparing a YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok post.

How to use Output History
  • Draft — generated but not reviewed yet.
  • Approved — you reviewed it and decided it is ready to use.
  • Used — you already posted it, filmed it, sent it, or used it somewhere.
Do not mark something Approved unless you have read it. Do not mark something Used unless it has actually been used.
Drafts = ideas waiting for review · Approved = ready-to-use packages · Used = already used content

What not to do
  • Do not post without reviewing.
  • Do not treat generated content as final.
  • Do not use the same caption everywhere without adjusting it.
  • Do not mark packages Used just because they were generated.
  • Do not enter private customer information.
