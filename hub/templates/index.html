How to use Output History
  • Draft — generated but not reviewed yet.
  • Approved — you reviewed it and decided it is ready to use.
  • Used — you already posted it, filmed it, sent it, or used it somewhere.
Do not mark something Approved unless you have read it. Do not mark something Used unless it has actually been used.
Drafts = ideas waiting for review · Approved = ready-to-use packages · Used = already used content
